About us: a printing company

The story of UPrinting: a vision for the perfectionists

UPrinting started in 2005 when e-commerce was finding its foothold online. Since then, we have grown and expanded to become the go-to printing company for small, medium and large business alike.

We understand the importance of exceeding expectations and getting it right the first time. That’s why every stage of the printing process—from design to checkout to shipping—delivers the highest quality every time.

Our Perfect Product

Before Printing

Before we start printing, we run each file through 13 checkpoints so your order is set up to print perfectly. Just the way you expect.

Printing & Finishing

During production, your job goes through 14 machine-run and human-led inspections to catch errors while there’s time to correct them.

Quality Control & Shipping

The final quality inspection has 6 points to ensure that your printed product meets our printing standards and is shipped on time.

CHOOSE YOUR DELIVERY DATE

Avoid unnecessary artist alley headaches and stressful wedding deadlines. We combine shipping & print time to take the guesswork out of ordering— just choose your delivery date at checkout and we’ll make sure your package arrives when you need it.

FREE HARD COPY PROOFS

Creating files can be complicated! Believe us, we’ve been there. To ensure your prints are just right, we offer an optional free hard copy proof every time you order prints. Once received, you can make any needed changes before printing. Don’t have time for a hard copy? We have an easy online-proofing service too!
